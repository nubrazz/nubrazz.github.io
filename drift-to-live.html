DRIFT TO LIVE

  • UnityBlenderLive Operation

Drift To Live is an exciting spin-off from "Private Server Game".
Players take on the role of a drifter navigating a chaotic world, earning money while dodging obstacles such as homing missiles, bombs, and flamethrowers. As the game progresses, players can unlock new cars and maps, further immersing themselves in the action-packed, drifter's journey.

Art Portfolio


Objective

Drift to Live is not only a fun and exciting game, but also an opportunity for personal growth as a developer.
Like many of my projects, this one is aimed at sharpening my skills and learning new things.
For example, I learned how cloud saving works and successfully implemented it in the game, as well as improved my 3D modeling skills. Even if the game doesn't have many players, I consider it a success as I have made progress and acquired new abilities.


Challenges


Drift to Live presented many challenges, but rather than feeling overwhelmed, I was energized by the opportunities to learn and grow. From game logic to backend services like cloud saving, I encountered a variety of obstacles, but I was determined to overcome them. Through hard work and perseverance, I was able to successfully tackle every challenge and ultimately triumph in the development of this project.

Overall, developing Drift To Live is such a fun learning experience and I see Drift To Live as a stepping stone for me to reach my potential.
