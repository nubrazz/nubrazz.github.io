PLAYGROUNDS

  • UnityExperimentation

Playgrounds projects are a collection of experimentation projects where I explore and test new concepts and technologies. I have many experimentation projects, but they can generally be grouped into 4 categories.


Mechanics

As the name suggests, projects in the Mechanics category focus on experimenting and testing new gameplay mechanics. One of my favorite "playground" projects in this category is the one where I experimented with a car controller. I enjoyed it so much that I ended up using the car controller in 3 different projects.


Graphics

Projects in the Graphics category have a diverse composition. Some of them are focused on experimenting with textures, some of them are experimenting with post-processing, but most of them are experimenting with Unity's Shadergraph. My favorite in this category is a water shader, where I created a fully-fledged water shader complete with ripple effects, foam, and refractions.


Backends

The Backends category is the most complex of all 4 categories. It includes projects where I experiment with
cloud save, servers, and other related topics. One of my biggest accomplishments in this category is successfully designing my own architecture for a game server backend.


Machine Learning

The Machine Learning category, as its name suggests, is filled with machine learning projects. I often enjoy experimenting with machine learning in Unity3D. I've conducted various experiments, from pathfinding to integrating machine learning into one of my own games.
