DOOM CAR PARTY

  • UnityBlenderFull Development

Doom Car Party is a casual multiplayer mobile game where players take on the role of a drifting master.
Navigate through a chaotic and fun gameplay experience while dodging obstacles such as explosive barrels and oil splashes. The game offers multiple game modes including races, survival and team mode to keep the game interesting.

Doom Car Party is my most ambitious project to date, boasting an enormous scope for a solo game developer.
All challenges like managing a highly-scalable game server only fuel my excitement to tackle them and strive for success.

Objective

I created Doom Car Party with a dual purpose: not only for players to enjoy, but also to push my boundaries in game development. I wanted to demonstrate my skills in game design, game mechanics, and level design, and to showcase my ability to create engaging and entertaining games.


Workflow


1. Conceptualization

The game development process begins with a wild imagination. From a wild imagination, I honed my vision and defined the desired gameplay experience.

2. Experimentation

Then I meticulously planned the necessary elements to bring the game to life. Experimentation is a vital aspect of the development process, during this stage I explored and tested various features to incorporate into the game and continually revised the game development plan.

3. Development

After thorough experimentation and achieving satisfactory results,
I embarked on the actual game development process.

4. Launch

Upon completion of the initial development phase, the game will be launched to the general public. Following the release, I will continue to provide updates, including new maps and cosmetic enhancements, to keep players engaged. Additionally, I will address any bugs or issues that arise to improve the overall player experience over time.



Current Proggress

Presently, Doom Car Party is in the development stage, with a focus on crafting an immersive gameplay experience, designing levels, and refining gameplay mechanics. I diligently work on the game every day to ensure steady progress, as I recognize that without discipline and consistency, the game with this ambitious scope might not be completed as a solo developer. Despite the challenges, I remain optimistic that I will be able to successfully deliver this ambitious project.
