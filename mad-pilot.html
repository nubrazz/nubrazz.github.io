MAD PILOT: STORMY NIGHT

  • UnityLive Operation

Mad Pilot: Stormy Night is an engaging arcade mobile game, and the first full-production project that I have worked on. Taking inspiration from my childhood favorite game "Platypus" players assume the role of a Mad Pilot, defending Earth from an alien invasion. With an upgrade system and new spaceships to unlock, players will face an array of alien ships and bosses, resulting in an exciting and unpredictable gameplay experience.


Art Portfolio


Objective

Initially, Mad Pilot began as a personal "playground" project, with my primary goal being to explore Unity3D and sharpen my C# programming skills. However, as the project progressed, it grew in scope and I realized that it would be a shame not to share it with the public. As a result, I refined the game and ultimately published it on the Google Play Store for others to enjoy.


Challenges

Mad Pilot presented many challenges throughout it's development, including integrating
AdMob as an ad provider and creating all of the necessary publishing materials such as trailers and posters.
Despite these challenges, I was able to overcome them and succeed
